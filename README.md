# بيت العزّ — Bait-elezz

**Your AI-powered interior design assistant** | **مساعدك الذكي للتصميم الداخلي**

---

## What is Bait-elezz?

Bait-elezz is a simple desktop and mobile app that uses artificial intelligence to help you design and decorate your home. You don't need any design experience — just answer a few questions about your room, and the app gives you professional recommendations in seconds.

The app works in both **Arabic** and **English**, and you can switch between them at any time.

---

## What can it do?

| Feature | What it helps with |
|---|---|
| **Wall Colors** | Suggests the best paint colors to match your walls |
| **Furniture Placement** | Tells you where to put each piece of furniture |
| **Curtain Color** | Recommends the best curtain color and fabric |
| **Furniture Dimensions** | Calculates the ideal size for each furniture piece |
| **Wood Planks** | Counts how many wood planks you need per color |
| **Upholstery Fabric** | Suggests fabric color and material for sofas and chairs |
| **Carpet Selection** | Recommends the right carpet color and material |
| **Runners & Accessories** | Helps you choose runners, vases, lighting, and mirrors |
| **Tableaux & Art** | Suggests artwork and how to hang it on your walls |

## How to get started

### Step 1 — Download the app

Go to the [Releases page](../../releases) and download the file for your device:

- **Windows**: Download the `.exe` file → double-click to install
- **Mac**: Download the `.dmg` file → drag the app to your Applications folder
- **Linux**: Download the `.AppImage` file → right-click → Properties → Allow executing as program → double-click to run
- **Android**: Download the `.apk` file → open it on your phone → tap Install (you may need to allow "Install from unknown sources" in your phone settings)

---

### Step 2 — Get your free API key

The app uses Claude AI to generate recommendations. You need a free API key from Anthropic.

1. Go to [console.anthropic.com](https://console.anthropic.com)
2. Create a free account
3. Click **API Keys** in the left menu
4. Click **Create Key** → give it any name → copy the key (it starts with `sk-ant-`)

> **Your key stays on your device and is never shared with anyone except Anthropic's AI service.**

---

### Step 3 — Enter your API key in the app

1. Open Bait-elezz
2. Click **Settings** (or الإعدادات) at the bottom of the sidebar
3. Paste your API key into the field
4. Click **Save API Key**

You only need to do this once.

---

### Step 4 — Use a feature

1. Pick a feature from the sidebar (e.g. "Wall Colors")
2. Fill in the simple form (e.g. pick your wall color from the color picker)
3. Optionally upload a photo of your room for more accurate advice
4. Click **Get AI Recommendation**
5. Read the detailed advice from the AI — it appears below the form

---

## Switching language

Click the flag button at the bottom of the sidebar to switch between **Arabic (العربية)** and **English**. The entire app switches instantly, including text direction (right-to-left for Arabic).

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need an internet connection?**
Yes — the app sends your room information to Claude AI to generate recommendations. Your data is sent directly to Anthropic's servers, nowhere else.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Nothing is stored on any server. Your API key is saved only on your own device.

**How much does it cost?**
The app itself is free. Anthropic charges a very small amount per AI request (usually less than $0.01 per recommendation). New accounts come with free credits to get started.

**The app shows "API key not configured" — what do I do?**
Go to Settings and make sure you've entered and saved your API key (see Step 2 and 3 above).

**Can I upload a photo?**
Yes! Most features let you upload a photo of your room. This helps the AI give you more accurate and personalized recommendations. Just drag and drop a photo, or click the upload area.

---

## Tips for best results

- Upload a clear, well-lit photo of your room when possible
- Be specific when filling in fields (e.g. list the exact furniture pieces you have)
- Try the same feature in both Arabic and English — the AI sometimes gives slightly different details in each language
- For **Wood Planks**, enter only the floor area you want to cover, not the full room size
